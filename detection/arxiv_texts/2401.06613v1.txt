WITH ENERGY BELOW GROUND STATE
YAN CUI AND BO XIA
ABSTRACT . In the previous work [ 6], we classiﬁed the solutions to a family of systems of
Klein-Gordon equations with non-negative energy below the ground state into two parts: one
blows up in ﬁnite time while the other extends to a global solu tion. In the present work, we
strengthen this result, showing that these global solution s are indeed scattering in the energy
space. Here we adapted Kenig-Merle’s concentration-compa ctness approach to the system.
