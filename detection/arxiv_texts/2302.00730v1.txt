Abstract. Axially symmetric solutions to the Navier-Stokes equation s in a
bounded cylinder are considered. On the boundary the normal component
of the velocity and he angular components of the velocity and vorticity are
assumed to vanish. If the norm of the initial swirl is suﬃcien tly small, then
the regularity of axially symmetric, weak solutions is show n. The key tool is
a new estimate for the stream function in certain weighted So bolev spaces.
