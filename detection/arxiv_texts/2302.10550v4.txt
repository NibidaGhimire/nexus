Abstract. LetAbe a simple separable nuclear monotracial C∗-algebra, and
letαbe an outer action of a ﬁnite abelian group Γ on A. In this paper,
we show that α⊗idWonA⊗ Wis approximately representable if and only
if the characteristic invariant of ˜ αis trivial, where Wis the Razak-Jacelon
algebra and ˜ αis the induced action on the injective II 1factorπτA(A)′′. As an
application of this result, we classify such actions up to co njugacy and cocycle
conjugacy. We also construct the model actions.
Introduction
In[13], Jonesgaveacompleteclassiﬁcationofﬁnite groupactionson the injective
II1factor up to conjugacy. This can be regarded as a generalization o f Connes’
classiﬁcation[2] of periodic automorphisms ofthe injective II 1factor. In this paper,
we study a C∗-analog of these results.
There exist some diﬃculties for the classiﬁcation of (amenable) grou p actions
on “classiﬁable” C∗-algebras because of K-theoretical obstructions. We refer the
reader to [11] for details. In spite of these diﬃculties, Gabe and Sza b´ o classiﬁed
outer actions of countable discrete amenable groups on Kirchberg algebras up to
cocycle conjugacy by equivariant KK-theory in [7]. This classiﬁcation can be re-
garded as a C∗-analog in Kirchberg algebras of Ocneanu’s classiﬁcation theorem
[25]. For stably ﬁnite classiﬁable C∗-algebras, such a classiﬁcation is an interesting
open problem.
The Razak-Jacelon algebra W([12] and [26]) is the simple separable nuclear
monotracial Z-stable C∗-algebra which is KK-equivalent to {0}. We regard Was
a monotracial analog of the Cuntz algebra O2. Indeed, if Ais a simple separable
nuclear monotracial C∗-algebra, then A⊗Wis isomorphic to Wby classiﬁcation
results in [1] and [5] (see also [23]). Let αbe an action on a simple separable
nuclear monotracial C∗-algebraA. Then the action α⊗idWonA⊗ Wis an
action on W. We call such a tensor product type action a W-type action . Note
that there exist no K-theoretical obstructions for W-type actions. Therefore we
can recognize diﬃculties due to stably ﬁniteness by studying W-type actions. In
[24], the author showed that if αis a strongly outer action of a countable discrete
amenable group, then W-type actions are unique up to cocycle conjugacy. (Note
that an action αof a discrete countable group on a simple separable monotracial
C∗-algebraAisstrongly outer if the induce action ˜ αbyαonπτA(A)′′is outer,
whereπτAis the Gelfand-Naimark-Segal (GNS) representation associated w ith the
unique tracial state τAonA.) This result can be regarded as a monotracial analog
of Szab´ o’s equivariant Kirchberg-Phillips type absorption theorem forO2[33]. In
