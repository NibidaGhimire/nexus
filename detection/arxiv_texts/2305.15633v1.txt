Abstract. In this paper we prove global well-posedness and scattering for the conformal, de-
focusing, nonlinear wave equation with radial initial data in the critical Sobolev space, for di-
mensions d≥4. This result extends a previous result proving sharp scatt ering in the three
dimensional case.
